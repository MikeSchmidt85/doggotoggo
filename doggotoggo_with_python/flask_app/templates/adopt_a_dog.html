Our mission to rescue, rehabilitate & re-home dogs never ends. We work together with multiple Rescue Centers in Orange County to assist in rescuing dogs from high kill shelters and terrible situations of neglect or abuse. So many dogs we rescue are on the verge of being euthanized at local Southern California city and county shelters. We like to bring in dog trainers and rehab specialists to work with our rescue dogs if they need it - especially those who have suffered injuries or abuse at the hands of previous owners. It’s a great way to build up a dog’s confidence, assimilate him to new owners and get him ready to meet his forever family!
